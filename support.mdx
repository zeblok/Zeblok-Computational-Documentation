The Zeblok support team is available to help with technical issues, custom node configurations, onboarding questions, and any other queries related to AI-MicroCloud.

## Contact the support team

Reach the appropriate team directly by email:

| Team | Email |
|---|---|
| Technical support | [support@zeblok.com](mailto:support@zeblok.com) |
| Sales | [sales@zeblok.com](mailto:sales@zeblok.com) |

For custom node configurations, modifications, or any platform-specific requirements, contact the technical team and describe your setup and requirements in as much detail as possible.

## Raise a ticket from AI-MicroCloud

You can raise a support ticket directly from within the AI-MicroCloud interface without leaving the platform. Click the **Support** button in the AI-MicroCloud UI to open a ticket form and submit your request.

## Preparing a support ticket

The more context you provide, the faster the support team can diagnose and resolve your issue.

<Tip>
  Include your component versions list when raising support tickets for faster resolution. After a successful cluster deployment, AI-MicroCloud automatically generates a list of all modules and their versions. Attach this list to your ticket.
</Tip>

Helpful information to include in your ticket:

- A clear description of the issue and steps to reproduce it
- The component versions list (available after cluster deployment)
- Any relevant log files (downloadable from the launcher on deployment failure)
- Your environment type (AWS, Azure, bare-metal, etc.)
- Your AI-MicroCloud version
