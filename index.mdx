## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an account">
    Sign up as an individual user or accept an invitation from your organization admin.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Spawn an Ai-WorkStation">
    Choose a GPU plan, select a namespace and datacenter, and launch a JupyterLab environment.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build and train your model">
    Use your preferred ML framework (PyTorch, TensorFlow, scikit-learn, and more) inside the notebook.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deploy as an AI API">
    Use the ZBL CLI to containerize your model and deploy it as a scalable REST API with one command.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Everyone currently has **Beta Access**, which unlocks all available plans. You can explore the full platform at no additional cost during the beta period.
</Note>
