---
title: "Sign in"
description: "Sign in to your Zeblok Computational account to access the dashboard and your workstations."
---

To sign in, navigate to [https://app.zbl-aws.zeblok.com/](https://app.zbl-aws.zeblok.com/).

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter your credentials">
    On the sign-in page, enter the email address and password you used when creating your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your sign-in method">
    You can sign in with your email and password, or use **Google** for single-click social sign-in if you registered with a Google account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete two-factor authentication (if enabled)">
    If you have two-factor authentication (2FA) turned on, you will be prompted to enter your verification code before proceeding. See [Two-factor authentication](/account/two-factor-auth) to set this up.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Land on the dashboard">
    After a successful sign-in you are redirected to the Zeblok dashboard. From here you can:

    - **Spawn Ai-WorkStation** — launch a GPU-powered JupyterLab environment
    - **Spawn MicroService** — deploy a containerized microservice
    - **Spawn Orchestration Add-on** — enable Slurm-as-a-Service for HPC workloads
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Don't have an account yet? Visit [Create an account](/create-an-account) to register as an individual user or accept an organization invitation.
</Info>
